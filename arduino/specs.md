## pinout:
* Seeed Studio Xiao esp32s3 sense:
  * DHT11:
    * pin 1: 3.3V
    * pin 2: D4
    * pin 3: GND
  * Battery: (pads on the back)
    * pin 1: battery +
    * pin 2: battery -
